How to set a realistic budget without missing opportunities

A strong budget should protect your comfort while still leaving room to act when the right home appears.

  • Start with monthly payment comfort, not just loan approval.
  • Include taxes, insurance, maintenance, and closing costs.
  • Keep reserves for repairs and moving expenses.
  • Know your stretch number before negotiations begin.

The best budget is one that lets you move confidently without creating stress after closing.

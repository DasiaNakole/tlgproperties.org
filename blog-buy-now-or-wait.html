Should you buy now or wait? A decision framework

The right answer depends less on headlines and more on your timeline, financial readiness, and local inventory.

  1. Review your time horizon for staying in the home.
  2. Check whether your cash reserves are healthy.
  3. Compare current inventory with your must-haves.
  4. Consider whether waiting improves your position or just delays your move.

A clear decision framework helps reduce noise and keeps your choice tied to real goals.

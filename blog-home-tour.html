5 things to do before your first home tour

Walking into showings with a plan helps you notice the right details and avoid wasting time on homes that do not fit your needs.

  1. Know your budget range. Decide what payment feels comfortable before you start looking.
  2. Write down must-haves. Bedrooms, layout, location, and commute matter more than decor.
  3. Bring your questions. Ask about age of systems, HOA details, repairs, and average utility costs.
  4. Look past staging. Focus on structure, flow, storage, and condition instead of furniture.
  5. Take notes after each stop. Homes blur together quickly, so capture what stood out right away.

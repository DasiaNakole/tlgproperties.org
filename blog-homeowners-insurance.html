What homeowners insurance actually covers

Most policies help cover the structure of the home, personal belongings, liability exposure, and temporary living costs after certain covered losses.

  • Dwelling coverage helps repair the physical house after covered damage.
  • Personal property coverage helps with belongings such as furniture and electronics.
  • Liability coverage may help if someone is injured on the property.
  • Loss-of-use coverage can help with temporary living expenses after a covered event.

Floods, earthquakes, and high-value items often require separate coverage or endorsements, so it is worth reviewing the policy carefully before closing.

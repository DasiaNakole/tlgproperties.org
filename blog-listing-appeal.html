Design updates that increase listing appeal quickly

Small visual changes can sharpen buyer interest before a property ever hits the market.

  • Refresh paint with light, clean neutrals.
  • Upgrade lighting where rooms feel dim or dated.
  • Declutter surfaces and reduce oversized furniture.
  • Style entry, kitchen, and primary suite first.
  • Use fresh landscaping for stronger curb appeal.

Buyers respond quickly to homes that feel bright, cared for, and easy to imagine themselves living in.
